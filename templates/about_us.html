About Us

Welcome to Page Portal (LMS) project - a Library Management System built to simplify the way libraries manage their collections, loans, and user interactions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly and efficient system that helps libraries manage their resources effortlessly. We aim to reduce administrative overhead and empower both librarians and patrons with reliable technology.

Key Features

  • User Authentication with Role Management
  • Book Cataloging and Advanced Search
  • Loan Issuance, Tracking, and Return Management
  • Responsive Design for a Seamless Experience on All Devices

Our Team

We are a passionate group of developers and library enthusiasts who believe in the power of technology to transform traditional library systems. Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement and innovation.
